DAY 1 - NETWORKING BASICS

1. Network:
A network is a group of connected devices that share data.

2. LAN:
Local Area Network - covers small area like home, school.

3. WAN:
Wide Area Network - covers large area like internet.

4. Switch:
A switch connects multiple devices in a LAN and allows communication.

5. Ping:
Ping is used to check connectivity between two devices.
